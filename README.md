## Arduino Project for CST1160: 
The automatic plant feeder, utilising these input and output modules:
- Soil Moisture Sensor
- Water Level Sensor
- LCD
- LED
- 5V Relay Module
- Water Pump Motor

## How it works:
The automatic plant feeder will utilise the above sensors to detect when the soil is dry, to trigger the water pump and water the plants automatically.
The water pump will only ever run when the soil is dry AND the water level is high enough.
There will be an LED light which will signal to the user when the water level is low.
The LCD will be used to monitor the moisture of the soil (DRY/ WET), the water level (as a %), as well as if the pump is ON or OFF.
